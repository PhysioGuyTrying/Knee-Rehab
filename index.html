Welcome to Knee Recovery

A 6-week progressive exercise program designed to help you safely strengthen and rehabilitate your knee.

📋 How the Program Works

Each week, you'll be given a set of exercises to complete daily. At the end of each week, you'll answer a few short questions about how your knee feels. If you're ready, you'll move on to more challenging exercises. If not, you'll repeat the current week — and that's completely fine. Everyone recovers at their own pace.

🎒 What You'll Need

  • A chair or sturdy surface for support
  • A towel (rolled up, for use during some exercises)
  • An exercise mat or comfortable floor surface — if you have difficulty getting up from the floor, you can perform floor exercises on your bed instead
  • Comfortable clothing that allows free movement

✅ Before You Begin

  • Perform the exercises on a flat, non-slip surface
  • Warm up gently before starting (a short walk is fine)
  • Follow along with each video at your own pace
  • Do not rush through the exercises — focus on control and form

🛑 When to Stop

Stop exercising and consult your healthcare provider if you experience:

  • Sharp or sudden pain in or around the knee
  • Significant swelling that doesn't go down
  • A feeling of instability or your knee "giving way"
  • Pain that worsens over the course of the week

⚠️ This program is not a substitute for professional medical advice. If you have any concerns about your condition, please speak with your physiotherapist or doctor before continuing.
